# Privacy Incident Reporting Procedure

## Purpose
To establish a process for reporting and escalating suspected privacy incidents.

## Procedure

### 1. Identify the Incident
Examples include:
- Unauthorized access to personal data
- Misdirected email containing sensitive information
- Lost or stolen device containing business data
- Improper sharing of confidential records

### 2. Report Immediately
Users must report the incident to:
- Information Security
- Management
- Designated incident response contact

### 3. Preserve Evidence
Do not delete emails, logs, files, or other evidence related to the incident.

### 4. Containment and Assessment
The appropriate team will:
- Assess impact
- Determine affected data
- Contain exposure
- Initiate response actions

### 5. Documentation
All incidents must be documented with:
- Date and time identified
- Reporter
- Description of the event
- Systems/data involved
- Actions taken

## Review
This procedure shall be reviewed annually.

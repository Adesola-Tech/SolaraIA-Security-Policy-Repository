# Policy Review and Maintenance Policy

## Policy Statement
SolaraIA security policies and standards must remain current, accurate, and aligned with business, legal, regulatory, and operational requirements.

## Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to define how security policies are reviewed, updated, approved, and communicated.

## Scope
This policy applies to all formal security policies, standards, procedures, and related governance documents maintained by SolaraIA.

## Requirements
- All policies must have an assigned owner
- Policies must be reviewed at least annually
- Material updates must be documented and approved
- Obsolete policies must be retired in a controlled manner
- Updated policies must be communicated to relevant stakeholders

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Policy Owners: Maintain document accuracy and coordinate updates
- Management: Review and approve policy updates
- Users: Comply with published policies
- Security Team: Support policy governance and alignment with security practices

## Version Control
Each policy document should include:
- Version number
- Effective date
- Last review date
- Approved by
- Change summary

## Review Cycle
Formal review shall occur at least once every 12 months.

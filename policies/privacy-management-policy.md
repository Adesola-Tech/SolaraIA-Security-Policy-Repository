# Privacy Management Policy

## Policy Statement
SolaraIA is committed to protecting personal, confidential, and sensitive data handled in the course of business operations.

## Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish privacy principles and data handling expectations that support regulatory compliance, protect individual privacy, and reduce organizational risk.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, third parties, systems, processes, and data repositories that collect, store, process, transmit, or dispose of sensitive information.

## Privacy Principles
SolaraIA will:
- Limit collection of personal data to legitimate business purposes
- Restrict access based on least privilege and need-to-know
- Protect data through technical and administrative safeguards
- Retain data only as long as necessary
- Dispose of data securely when no longer required

## Data Handling Requirements
Sensitive data must:
- Be classified appropriately
- Be encrypted in transit and at rest where applicable
- Be shared only with authorized individuals
- Be stored only in approved locations

## Access Control
Access to private or sensitive information must be granted based on job responsibilities and reviewed periodically.

## Incident Reporting
Any suspected privacy breach, data exposure, or unauthorized disclosure must be reported immediately through SolaraIA’s incident reporting process.

## Compliance
SolaraIA will support compliance with applicable legal, contractual, and regulatory privacy obligations.

## Enforcement
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, access revocation, legal consequences, and corrective action plans.

## Review Cycle
This policy shall be reviewed annually or when major regulatory or operational changes occur.

# Acceptable Use Policy

## Policy Statement
SolaraIA provides employees, contractors, and authorized users with access to information systems, networks, devices, and data to support business operations. All users are expected to use these resources responsibly, ethically, and securely.

## Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to define acceptable use of SolaraIA technology resources and protect the organization from risks including malware, data loss, unauthorized access, and legal exposure.

## Scope
This policy applies to:
- Employees
- Contractors
- Temporary staff
- Third parties with authorized access to SolaraIA systems

## Acceptable Use Requirements
Users must:
- Use company systems primarily for authorized business purposes
- Protect credentials and not share accounts
- Follow security requirements when accessing systems remotely
- Report suspicious activity, phishing attempts, or security incidents promptly

Users must not:
- Install unauthorized software
- Attempt to bypass security controls
- Use company resources for illegal, harmful, or inappropriate activities
- Access, modify, or distribute data without authorization

## Monitoring
SolaraIA reserves the right to monitor, log, and review usage of company systems, networks, email, and internet resources to ensure compliance and protect organizational assets.

## Enforcement
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract, legal action, and revocation of system access.

## Exceptions
Exceptions to this policy must be documented and approved through the formal exception process.

## Review Cycle
This policy shall be reviewed annually or upon significant business, regulatory, or technology changes.

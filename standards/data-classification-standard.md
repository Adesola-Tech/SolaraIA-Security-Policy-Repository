# Data Classification Standard

## Purpose
To define how SolaraIA classifies and handles information based on sensitivity and business impact.

## Classification Levels

### Public
Information approved for public release.

### Internal
Information intended for internal business use only.

### Confidential
Sensitive business information that could cause harm if disclosed without authorization.

### Restricted
Highly sensitive information requiring strict access control, such as regulated data, personal data, or financial records.

## Handling Requirements
- Public: No special restrictions
- Internal: Share within authorized business personnel
- Confidential: Restrict access and protect during storage and transmission
- Restricted: Apply highest protections including encryption, limited access, and enhanced monitoring

## Labeling
Where practical, documents and records should be labeled according to classification.

## Review
This standard must be reviewed annually.
